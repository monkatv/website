Weekly chat analytics for select Twitch streamers — emotes, chatters,
copypastas, and per-VOD breakdowns. Updated every Monday with the past week's data.

The numbers come from public Twitch VOD chat logs; nothing private, nothing live.
The pipeline is open-source on [GitHub](https://github.com/monkatv/tcs), and the
raw weekly JSON for each streamer is served at
`/api/{streamer}/{YYYY-MM-DD}.json` if you want to chart it yourself.
